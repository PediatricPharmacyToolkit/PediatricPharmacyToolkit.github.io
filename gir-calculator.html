💡 Helpful Notes & Tips
  • Double-check units before adjusting therapy.
  • Typical neonatal GIR targets range from 4–8 mg/kg/min.
  • Monitor serum glucose closely when titrating.
  • Quick equation = (Dextrose % × mL/hr) ÷ (kilograms × 6).
